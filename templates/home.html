Welcome to our dream come true - Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Bharatpur (Raj) - It is one of the integrated school situated at Chhonakarwara, at a distance of 50 km from the district headquarter Bharatpur.

The Vidyalaya Aims are:

  • To provide good quality education with modern techniques to the talented children predominantly from the rural areas without regard to their economic conditions.
  • To ensure that all students of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya may attain a reasonable level of competency in three language formula i.e. in Hindi, English and Gujrati.
  • To serve, in the district, as focal points for improvements in quality of school education through sharing of experiences and facilities.
